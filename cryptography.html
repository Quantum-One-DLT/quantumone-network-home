Quantum Computing Cybersecurity Preparedness Act

Signed and enacted by US President Biden on December 21 2022, US Congress deemed cryptography essential to the national security and functioning of the economy of the United States. The Act stresses the need for a strategy for the migration of information technology of the Federal Government to post-quantum cryptography and government wide and industry wide approach to post-quantum cryptography should prioritize developing applications, hardware intellectual property, and software that can be easily updated to support cryptographic agility.
